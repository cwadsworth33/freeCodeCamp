About Me

Hi! My name is Christian Wadsworth. I am an aspiring web developer / software engineer. I am currently in my senior year at Lehigh University where I am studying electrical engineering and minoring in computer science. In my free time, I enjoy playing tournament chess, board games, and hiking.


Along with programming skills I have picked up through Lehigh's ECE program (Java, Matlab, Arduino, etc), I have been learning front-end web development in my spare time with freeCodeCamp since September 2017. This experience moved me to minor in computer science in my last semester at Lehigh, where I will be learning about databases, software engineering, AI, and web programming. I am looking forward to creating and learning more about software in the future.


My short-term goal is to obtain employment developing web applications.
